Our Services

Our ServiceS

Air, Sea, Road Transport, Warehousing, SEZ Solution, Distribution, and Fulfillment.

Air Transport

  • International and Domestic Flights: We partner with leading airlines to provide reliable and efficient air freight services across East Africa and globally.
  • Custom Clearance: Our experts handle all customs formalities to ensure smooth and timely clearance of your shipments.
  • Temperature-Controlled Transport: We offer specialized solutions for transporting temperature-sensitive goods, such as pharmaceuticals and perishables.

Sea Transport

  • Ocean Freight: We arrange ocean freight services to and from major ports in East Africa, including Mombasa, Dar es Salaam, and Durban.
  • Container Shipping: We offer a variety of container options, including standard, reefer, and open-top containers, to suit your cargo requirements.
  • Break Bulk Shipping: We can handle shipments that do not fit into standard containers, ensuring efficient and cost-effective transportation.

Road Transport

  • Trucking: We provide reliable trucking services within East Africa, ensuring timely delivery of your goods.
  • Rail Transport: We leverage the expanding railway networks in East Africa to offer efficient and cost- effective land transportation options.
  • Intermodal Transport: We combine different modes of transport to optimize the movement of goods, ensuring the most efficient and cost-effective solution.

SEZ Solutions

  • Tax Incentives: Enjoy favorable tax rates and incentives to reduce your operational costs.
  • Simplified Customs Procedures: Benefit from streamlined customs clearance processes, reducing delays and costs.
  • Infrastructure Development: Access world-class infrastructure, including roads, railways, and utilities, within SEZs.
  • Skilled Workforce: Tap into a pool of skilled labor available within SEZs.
  • Strategic Location: Benefit from the strategic location of SEZs, providing access to key markets and transportation hubs.

On-Demand Warehousing

  • Flexible Storage Solutions: We provide customizable storage options to accommodate various product types and volumes.
  • Real-time Inventory Tracking: Our advanced systems enable you to monitor your inventory levels in real- time, ensuring optimal stock management.
  • Pick and Pack Services: Our experienced team efficiently handles order picking and packaging to minimize errors and expedite delivery.
  • Value-Added Services: We offer additional services such as kitting, bundling, and custom labeling to enhance your product presentation.

Distribution

  • Efficient Routing: We utilize advanced algorithms to optimize delivery routes, reducing transportation costs and transit times.
  • Multimodal Transportation: We offer flexible transportation options, including air, sea, and road freight, to meet your specific shipping requirements.
  • Last-Mile Delivery: We provide reliable last-mile delivery services to ensure your products reach your customers on time.

Order Fulfillment

  • Order Processing: Our streamlined order processing system ensures accurate and timely fulfillment of your orders.
  • Inventory Management: We maintain optimal inventory levels to minimize stockouts and ensure product availability.
  • Quality Control: We implement rigorous quality control measures to ensure the integrity of your products.
  • Returns Management: We handle returns efficiently, minimizing customer inconvenience and maximizing your revenue.

Order Consolidation for Modern and General Trade

  • Reduced shipping costs: By consolidating orders, businesses can reduce the number of shipments required, leading to significant savings on transportation costs.
  • Improved efficiency: Consolidation can streamline the logistics process by reducing handling time and improving warehouse utilization.
